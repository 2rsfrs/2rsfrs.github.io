Water Country USA

A fun water based theme park that has many fun water slides, a lazy river, and a wave pool! It also has gift shops and places to eat. If you are handicapped, you don't have to worry. You can do things too! You can rest in a Cabana, your hotel, or celebrate your birthday! If you are a parent and you live far away from this place, you can stay at a hotel nearby and there is parking near by. You can find these hotels here!

Who can attend and when?

Well, anyone can attend. Wether your a baby, kid, adult, or senior! Everyone will have fun doing something. The park is open from May 21 to Setember 5. It's open near the summer and not fall-spring because it will be cold outside at those times and everyone in the water will be freezing. The opening time is at 10:00 AM and it closes at 5:00 pm to 8:00 pm. It is different every day.

What if i'm handicapped?

Well if you are handicapped, it is suggested you call the service associate ahead of time so they can prepare for your visit to the park. There are pools in the park so if your handicapped then you can go in the water with your kids or your grand children. It is recommended you shouldn't go on thrill rides if you have health problems. You could also rest in a rented cabana and take a nice nap in the summer breeze.

What can I eat and what are things I can do?

You can eat at the various food vendors as seen on the map on the top. You can celebrate your birthday and enjoy the wave pool, the lazy river, the water slides, shopping, or rest in a cabana. The places you can eat are at Pinky's, Daddy O's, Coolsville, Catatina Grille, Pit Stop, Launch Pad Pub and Sub, Hot spot cafe, Way out candy shop, and many more!
