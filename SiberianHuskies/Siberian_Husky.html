Siberian Huskies!

Siberian Huskies are dogs that were bred by the Chukchi tribe in the past. Siberian Huskies have 2 coats/layers so they won't be cold if they
live in the cold. They have eye that look like almonds. They are like wolves because they howl. They are intelligent and can jump really high!
If you want to adopt a siberian husky, you will need a tall fence so they can't jump over it to escape to chase a cat.
